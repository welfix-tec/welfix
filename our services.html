Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes.

How long does it take to complete a website project?
The timeline for a website project typically ranges from 4-8 weeks, depending on the complexity of the project, the number of pages, and the responsiveness of the client in providing content and feedback.
Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?
Yes, we offer various ongoing support and maintenance packages to ensure your website remains up-to-date, secure, and continues to perform optimally after launch.
Can I update my website content myself?
Absolutely! We build websites with user-friendly content management systems that allow you to easily update content, add blog posts, and make other changes without technical knowledge.
How do you measure the success of social media campaigns?
We use a variety of metrics including engagement rate, follower growth, website traffic from social media, conversion rates, and ROI. We provide monthly reports with detailed analytics.
